Online Voting Systems

Online voting systems are a modern way of letting people vote using the internet instead of paper ballots. The goal is to make voting more convenient and accessible. With online voting, eligible individuals can cast their votes from anywhere with an internet connection. This approach aims to overcome traditional barriers like distance or mobility issues.
